Overview Counter ("the App") is a counting and practice-tracking app for iPhone and Apple Watch. We respect your privacy. This policy explains how the App handles your information. Data We Collect We do not collect any personal data. The App does not require an account, does not connect to any server we operate, and does not include any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking tools. How Your Data Is Stored All data you create in the App — counter names, values, schedules, reminder times, and completion history — is stored only on your own devices. Data is synchronized between your iPhone and Apple Watch directly through Apple's device-to-device connectivity. This synchronization happens locally between your paired devices and does not pass through any server controlled by us. Notifications The App can schedule local reminders on your device. These notifications are generated and delivered entirely on your device. No notification content is sent to or through any external server. Data Sharing We do not share, sell, or transmit your data to anyone, because we never collect or receive it in the first place. Your Control You can delete any counter and its associated data at any time within the App. Deleting the App from your devices removes all associated local data. Children's Privacy The App does not collect data from anyone, including children. Changes to This Policy If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this page with a new "Last updated" date.
